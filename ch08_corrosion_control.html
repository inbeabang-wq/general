Nature of Corrosion📄 PDF p.325

부식(Corrosion)은 금속이 환경과 전기화학 반응으로 산화되어 열화되는 현상입니다. 성립에는 양극·음극·전해질·전기 경로 네 요소가 필요합니다.Corrosion is the electrochemical oxidation and deterioration of metal by its environment. It requires four elements: an anode, a cathode, an electrolyte, and an electrical path.

📖 핸드북 원문 정리 (FAA-H-8083-30B p.325) ▼
Corrosion is the electrochemical oxidation and deterioration of metal by its environment. It requires four elements: an anode, a cathode, an electrolyte, and an electrical path. Each metal shows characteristic products — white powder on aluminum, red rust on steel, white deposits on magnesium. Moisture, salt, and contaminants accelerate corrosion.

지속적 인장응력을 받는 부품(부싱 압입 구멍 주변 등).
In a component under continual tensile stress, e.g., around pressed-in bushings.

Q19알루미늄 표면부식 제거·처리 방법은?How do you remove and treat surface corrosion on aluminum?

강모 브러시로 제거→크롬산/전환코팅으로 중화→도장 보호.
Remove with a bristle brush, neutralize with chromic acid or a conversion coating, then paint.

Q20구조용 알루미늄에 부식이 생기지 않게 하는 것은?What keeps corrosion from forming on structural aluminum?

산화피막 또는 알루미늄 클래딩.
An oxide coating or aluminum cladding.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B
Q21고응력 부품의 녹(rust) 제거법은?How may rust be removed from a highly stressed part?

글래스비드 블라스팅, 부드러운 연마지 연마, 버핑 컴파운드.
Glass-bead blasting, careful polishing, or buffing compound.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B
Q22피아노힌지의 부식을 최소화하는 방법은?How should corrosion be minimized at piano hinges?

청결·건조 유지 후 저점도 수분방지제로 윤활.
Keep clean and dry, lubricate with a low-viscosity moisture-dispersing agent.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B

MODEL ANSWER · 모범답안
알루미늄 울/알루미늄 와이어 브러시, 심하면 로터리 파일.
Aluminum wool or aluminum wire brushes; a rotary file for severe corrosion.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B
Q24투명 플라스틱 풍방·창문 세척은?What is used to clean transparent plastic windshields?
MODEL ANSWER · 모범답안
순한 비누와 깨끗한 물.
Mild soap and plenty of clean water.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B

탄산수소나트륨 용액.
A solution of bicarbonate of soda and water.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B
Q26쏟아진 니카드 전해질의 중화제는?What neutralizes spilled Ni-Cad electrolyte?
MODEL ANSWER · 모범답안
붕산 용액 또는 식초.
A solution of boric acid and water, or vinegar.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B
Q27도프 전 천의 기름 제거 용제는?What solvent removes grease from fabric before doping?
MODEL ANSWER · 모범답안
MEK(메틸에틸케톤) 또는 래커 시너.
Methyl ethyl ketone (MEK) or lacquer thinner.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B
Q28알루미늄 부품의 양극산화 표면을 보수하는 것은?What repairs an anodized aluminum surface?
MODEL ANSWER · 모범답안
알로다인(Bonderite) 같은 화학전환피막.
A chemical conversion coating such as Alodine (Bonderite).

Q29마그네슘 합금의 표면부식 제거 도구는?What removes surface corrosion from magnesium alloy?
MODEL ANSWER · 모범답안
뻣뻣한 돈모(hog-bristle) 브러시.
A stiff hog-bristle brush.
REFERENCE: AMT-G, FAA-H-8083-30B

뜨거운 아마인유(linseed oil)를 채웠다 빼낸다.
Filled with hot linseed oil, then drained.

도프가 구조에 천을 달라붙게 하지 않도록.
To keep the fabric from sticking to the structure.

A mildewcide is mixed into the first coat of dope.

Q33폴리에스터 합성 천은 어떻게 수축시키나?How is polyester synthetic fabric shrunk?

다리미나 가열기로 열을 가한다.
With heat from an iron or heated blower.

Q34나무 스파를 투명 바니시로 마감하는 이유는?Why are wooden spars finished with transparent varnish?

It softens and swells, pulling away from the surface.

도료를 너무 많이 뿌려 막이 두꺼움.
Too much paint; the film is too thick.

Q38도프 표면의 블러싱(blushing)을 없애는 방법은?How to remedy blushing on a freshly sprayed doped surface?

리타더+시너 혼합액을 얇게 미스트 도포 후 건조.
Spray a light mist of retarder and thinner, then let dry.

Wet the floor first; dry sweeping can cause a static-electricity fire.

도프의 시너가 에나멜을 침투해 팽윤시킨다.
The thinner penetrates the enamel and causes it to swell.

Zinc chromate, wash primer, and epoxy primer.
